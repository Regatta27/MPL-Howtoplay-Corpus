Now you’re the newest MPL manager, let’s have a look at what the job involves.

Get your squad together

You might be the boss, but you are nothing without your players!

  • Football on Mars is a little different to the game on Earth - yes there are humans, but we also have Martians, astronauts and synthetic players too
  • Your squad can be built from any combination of genesis characters you own, plus Gaffer’s handy latest invention - the Synthetics
  • If you already own an MPL these players will automatically pull through into your player roster as soon as you have the wallet containing those players connected
  • If you don’t have enough MPLs for a full squad (or any MPLs for that matter) you have two options
    1. Head on over to Opensea and make a signing that will leave the competition trembling in their boots. (The in-game player leaderboards may help you determine exactly which player might perfectly fill that open spot).
    1. Or here to help you round out your squad is the state of the art Synth v1.01 by Gaffer Industries, a machine designed purely to play football on Mars*. Meaning you don’t need a full squad of (or indeed any) MPLs to play.

      *Some recent supply chain issues mean that when generated some Synths come out better than others! Will you be one of the lucky managers to draw an excellent Synth?

  • Draft in as many Synths as you need. Each team will be able to generate 15 Synths to choose from.

Look out for this module when creating your team to generate your synths >>>

Bring out the best in your players

The Gaffer loves a trier, but we can’t deny that some players have more natural talent than others.

There are 8 skill levels on Mars, from struggling through to god like. Each skill level has an associated number of skill points available to allocate across seven different skill attributes - passing, defending, playmaking, finishing, goalkeeping, stamina and wing play.

Synthetics (naturally) aren’t quite as good as their organic counterparts, hence having slightly fewer skill points at each skill level. Gaffer hasn’t quite nailed replicating the higher levels of human or martian natural talent either, and has only managed to create Synthetics up to excellent skill level to date (with some not even making the novice grade!).

Generate skill attributes

Whatever raw materials you are working with, it’s up to you as manager to bring out the best in them. What skills will you prioritise when generating skill attributes? This is no small decision as a manager, so think carefully about what skill attributes you value in each player.

Position is also key, will you choose to play your star midfielder in centre midfield, or leverage their skills on the wings?

  • When generating skill attributes, managers will be able to choose the type of player they want to generate - forward, midfielder, defender or goalkeeper. Gaffer Systems will then generate skill attributes accordingly.
  • You will be able to continue to re-generate skill attributes until you’re happy with the blend of skills that your player has, at which point lock and load them, and your player is ready to join the squad.
  • You can play any player with any combination of skill attributes in any position. There is no one right way to design your team, you need to use you managerial skills to set your team up for success, and consider how these customisation choices will intersect with your strategic decisions.
  • Re-generate skill points - feel like you haven’t quite nailed your skill attribute mix across the team? Every first and third Sunday of the month* there will be a 24 hour window where you have the opportunity to come back and re-generate your players skill attributes.

    *How these regeneration windows work will likely evolve as we introduce the more structured leagues and tournaments, with for example, managers only being able to re-generate their player skill attributes in between tournaments. However, during Head 2 Heads we’ll open these up every couple of weeks.

^You can easily swap a player’s position around once you have them in place.
